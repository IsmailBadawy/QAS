About Us

QAS was established in 2014, based in Cairo, Egypt.We provide a complete consultation and software solutions to the healthcare organizations in order to enhance the provided services and to facilitate the process of quality management system implementation.

QAS Our solutions and services comply with the national and international quality and accreditation standard/s as well as the applicable guide lines.
